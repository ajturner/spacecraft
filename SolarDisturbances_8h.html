 Solar radiation pressure environmental disturbance force function.Another non-conservative disturbance force, like atmospheric drag, is due to the fact that light photons can impart a force on an absorbing or reflecting body. The force of these photons is usually very low, but can vary largely between eclipse, based on the body, and during solar storms. The solar-radiation pressure is even the basis for such spacecraft propulsion designs as solar sails, and so should be used for accurate models. More...
